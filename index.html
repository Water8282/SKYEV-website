HOW LONG ARE YOUR CUSTOMER WARRANTIES?

Each EPIC golf cart sold by an authorized dealer comes with a 3-year manufacturer warranty from the date of sale. ECO Lithium Batteries are warranted for 8 years from your date of purchase (3 years full replacement value, and 5 years prorated).

CAN I DRIVE MY GOLF CART ON THE STREET?

Yes! All EPIC golf carts are street legal throughout most of the United States. Your local authorized dealer can provide you with more details pertaining to your state’s law.

CAN I HAVE ANOTHER DEALER WORK ON MY CART?

Yes. Any authorized EPIC golf cart dealer can perform warranty work. However, your original dealer is responsible for warranty work after the sale. If you decide to get warranty work done outside of the dealer who sold you your cart, the parts will be supplied to the non-selling dealer from the Manufacturer, however, the non-selling dealer may or may not charge labor. Work performed outside of an authorized EPIC golf cart dealer or service factory center may void the limited warranty.
